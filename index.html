Welcome to RedHed

Redhed is an indie band based out of Vaughan, Ontario.

Blending elements of rock, indie, and punk, Redhed aims to fill the absence of local bands, venues, or role models in the suburbs

Redhed isn't just a band, but an answer to that silence.
